Overview

Spain is a European country located on the Iberian Peninsula. It is known for its rich culture, history, festivals, and Mediterranean lifestyle.

Government

Spain is a parliamentary constitutional monarchy. The King is the head of state, while the Prime Minister is the head of government.

Population

Spain has a population of around 47 million people. Major cities include Madrid, Barcelona, and Valencia.

Economy

The Spanish economy is diverse, with tourism, agriculture, and manufacturing playing major roles. Spain is one of the most visited countries in the world.

Climate & Agriculture

Spain has a Mediterranean climate with hot summers and mild winters. It is a leading producer of olives, wine, citrus fruits, and vegetables.
