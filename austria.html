Austria is a federal parliamentary republic with a president as head of state and a chancellor as head of government. The legislature is bicameral, with the National Council and Federal Council. Austria has nine federal states, each with its own government for local administration. The judiciary is independent. Democracy ensures free elections, civil liberties, and political pluralism. Major political parties include the Austrian People's Party and Social Democratic Party. Policies focus on economic development, education, healthcare, infrastructure, and social welfare, ensuring stable governance and strong regional representation.
