Russia is the largest country in the world, spanning Eastern Europe and Northern Asia. It shares borders with 14 countries and has diverse landscapes, including tundra, forests, mountains, and vast plains.

Russia has a population of about 146 million people, making it one of the most populous countries in the world. Moscow is the capital and the most populous city.

Russia is a federal semi-presidential republic. The President is the head of state, while the Prime Minister is the head of government.

Russia has a variety of climates, but much of the country experiences very cold winters. Siberia is known for its extreme cold, while southern regions have milder conditions.
