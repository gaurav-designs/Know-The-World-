Overview

Italy is a European country famous for its history, culture, art, and cuisine. It is home to ancient Roman ruins, Renaissance art, and beautiful landscapes.

Government

Italy is a parliamentary republic. The President is the head of state, while the Prime Minister is the head of government.

Population

Italy has a population of around 59 million people. Major cities include Rome, Milan, Naples, and Florence.

Economy

The Italian economy is driven by fashion, automobiles, tourism, and agriculture. It is one of the largest economies in Europe.

Climate & Agriculture

Italy has a Mediterranean climate with warm summers and mild winters. It is a leading producer of wine, olives, pasta wheat, and cheese.
