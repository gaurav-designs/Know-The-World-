Overview

Mexico is a country in North America known for its rich culture, ancient civilizations, and diverse landscapes ranging from deserts to tropical beaches.

Government

Mexico is a federal republic with a President serving as both head of state and government.

Population

Mexico has a population of over 126 million people. Major cities include Mexico City (the capital), Guadalajara, and Monterrey.

Economy

The Mexican economy is driven by manufacturing, oil production, agriculture, and tourism. It is also known for trade with the United States under the USMCA agreement.

Climate & Agriculture

Mexico has diverse climates: deserts in the north, tropical jungles in the south, and mild highland areas. Key crops include corn, beans, coffee, and avocados.
